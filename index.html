Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The kindergarten curriculum is designed to nurture the physical, cognitive, social, emotional, and creative development of children aged 3 to 6 years. It provides a safe, engaging, and stimulating environment that encourages curiosity, exploration, and a lifelong love for learning.

Elementary School

The elementary school curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive and well-rounded education that nurtures the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical development of children. It introduces foundational academic knowledge and skills while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a sense of responsibility.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle school curriculum is designed to bridge the transition between elementary school and high school, addressing the unique developmental needs of students aged 10–16 years. It emphasizes academic, social, emotional, and physical growth while fostering independence, critical thinking, and responsibility.
